Civic Vision: The Unity Accord & The Empty Chair

The global orphan crisis cannot be solved by institutions, governments, churches, or charities working in isolation. Too many vulnerable children grow up without representation, protection, belonging, or support, and too many young people age out of orphanages into homelessness, exploitation, and uncertainty. The problem is not merely a lack of resources; it is a lack of connectedness, coordinated responsibility, and enduring relationships.

The vision of The Unity Accord is to rewrite the orphan narrative by building a global movement that unites churches, communities, businesses, governments, and development partners to ensure that every child has a place to belong and every young person has a pathway to thrive. We aim to build "A Home Beyond the Orphanage" through transitional homes, mentorship, trauma-informed care, and economic empowerment.

🪑
The Symbol of The Empty Chair

Millions of vulnerable children are absent from the rooms where decisions about their futures are made. Our mission is to ensure that someone always occupies that chair on their behalf—demonstrating that in both business and society, the future belongs to those who learn to unite around a shared purpose.
